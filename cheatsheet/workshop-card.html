Workshop Card — Run a World-Class AI on Your Laptop Five steps, ~45 minutes, one outcome: your own private AI running on your machine.

1

Install · Do tonight · ~20 min, mostly waiting

DomacOSTerminal:
git clone https://github.com/danielpaulai/Run-Worldclass-computer-locally
cd Run-Worldclass-computer-locally
./preflight.sh && ./setup.sh
DoWindowsPowerShell:
git clone https://github.com/danielpaulai/Run-Worldclass-computer-locally
cd Run-Worldclass-computer-locally
.\preflight.ps1 ; .\setup.ps1
SeeA green "Setup complete" banner at the end. Done.
2

First chat — "is it alive?" · In class · 3 min

Do
./ai local-ai ask "hi in 3 words"
See~15 seconds later: something like Hello! Nice meeting.
3

Summarize a known doc — the "wow" · 5 min

Do
./ai summarize tldr README.md
SeeA clean 3-sentence summary of the repo. Accurate. Fast.
4

Summarize YOUR document — the conversion moment · 20 min

Do
./ai summarize tldr ~/Documents/your-file.pdf
./ai summarize actions ~/Documents/your-file.pdf
./ai local-writer draft "Reply to this email politely declining..."
SeeUseful output on YOUR stuff. Nothing leaves your machine.
5

Prove it's private — the moment that sticks · 2 min

DoTurn off WiFi. Airplane mode. Then:
./ai local-ai ask "Still working without internet?"
SeeThe model answers. Offline. That's the whole point.
