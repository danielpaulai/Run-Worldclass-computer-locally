I built an AI that runs entirely on your laptop. No cloud. No subscription. No data ever leaves your machine.

It's called **Purely Personal**.

Here's the problem it solves.

Every consultant, lawyer, accountant, and coach I know has a folder of documents they won't paste into ChatGPT. Client contracts. Session notes. Financial statements. Things they signed NDAs about.

That's the wrong half of the value.

→ A coach pastes her session notes after a hard call. Asks "what commitments did the client make and what did they dodge?" Gets both lists.

None of that data left their laptop.

It costs $0. It runs on a plane. It has no login.
