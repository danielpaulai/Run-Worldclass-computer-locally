## Rules for the post

- No jargon. The word "leverage" does not appear.
- No "AI-powered synergy."
- Specific numbers beat adjectives. (*"12 seconds"* > *"fast"*)
- One sentence per line. Scanners don't read paragraphs on LinkedIn.
- Every claim has a receipt somewhere (open source, verifiable privacy, real use cases).
