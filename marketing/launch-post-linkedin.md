## Voice rules (memorize)

- Start with "I think," "So I've been thinking about this," or "You know what nobody says?" Reflective, not announcing.
- Short standalone lines. One thought per line.
- Use "Yeah, so this is what I want you to take away from this" as the pivot.
- One-word power lines. "You." "No." "Exactly."
- Ask questions that answer themselves. *"So what do you do with those? You do them by hand."*
- Pattern. Use X, but never Y. (*"Use AI for the public stuff. But for the private stuff..."*)
- End with a P.S. that asks the reader a specific question, not a generic CTA.

## Words to avoid

- "Leverage"
- "Unlock"
- "Revolutionary"
- "Game-changer"
- "Synergy"
- "Transformative"
- "AI-powered"
- "Cutting-edge"
- Em dashes
- En dashes

If you catch yourself writing any of those, start the sentence over.
